Slime Rancher 2 Info

Hello!

What?

Slime Rancher 2 is a sequel to the award-winning, smash-hit original that has been enjoyed by over 15 million fans worldwide. The game continues the adventures of Beatrix LeBeau as she journeys to Rainbow Island, a mysterious land brimming with ancient technology, unknown natural resources, and an avalanche of wiggling, jiggling, new slimes to discover. This website contains details on the slimes, characters, food, locations, toys and items that can be found within Slime Rancher 2.

Why?

I created this website in order to improve and maintain my web development skills. With it recently coming to PS5, I have been playing the game Slime Rancher 2. So why not make a website on that? I have been meaning to try out and experiment with JSONs, so I figured making an info-based website was a good way to do so. I am also trying out some new things in CSS and JS, yippee!

Who?

I am a soon to be second year Computing Science student at Heriot Watt University. I am currently on my summer holidays :]
